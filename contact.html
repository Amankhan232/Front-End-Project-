Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM, 7 days a week. Online ordering is available 24x7, and you can schedule orders for the next day.

What is your minimum order amount?

There's no minimum order amount. However, free delivery is available on orders above ₹299. Orders below this amount have a delivery charge of ₹40.

Do you offer refunds for cancelled orders?

Yes, we offer full refunds for orders cancelled before preparation begins. If the order is already being prepared, we can offer store credit or partial refund based on the situation.

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely! You can customize spice levels, add special instructions, or request modifications. Just mention your preferences in the order notes or call us directly.

Do you cater to dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer various options for different dietary needs including vegan, gluten-free, and low-spice options. Please mention your requirements when ordering.
