About Delicious

Bringing authentic Indian flavors to your doorstep with passion, tradition, and love for over a decade

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Chef Rajesh Kumar, Delicious began as a small family kitchen with a big dream - to share the authentic flavors of Indian cuisine with food lovers everywhere.

What started as a humble venture has grown into one of the most trusted names in Indian food delivery, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the city.

Our journey is rooted in tradition, powered by innovation, and driven by our unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To preserve and share the rich culinary heritage of India by delivering authentic, high-quality meals that bring families together and create memorable dining experiences.

We believe that great food has the power to connect people, celebrate culture, and create lasting memories. Every dish we prepare is a testament to this belief.

Our commitment extends beyond just food - we strive to provide exceptional service, maintain the highest standards of hygiene, and contribute positively to our community.

Our Core Values

Authenticity

We stay true to traditional recipes and cooking methods, ensuring every dish reflects the genuine taste of Indian cuisine.

Quality

Only the freshest ingredients and finest spices make it to our kitchen. We never compromise on quality.

Community

We're more than a restaurant - we're part of the community, supporting local suppliers and giving back whenever possible.

Reliability

Consistent quality, timely delivery, and dependable service - that's our promise to every customer.

Safety

Maintaining the highest standards of food safety and hygiene is our top priority in everything we do.

Customer First

Every decision we make is guided by one question: How can we better serve our customers?
